FAQs الأسئلة الشائعة

1. What types of buggies are available?

We offer single-seater, two-seater, four-seater (family/group), premium high-performance, and self-drive buggies to suit all preferences and skill levels.

2. Do I need a valid driving license?

A valid driving license is required for self-drive and rental options. Guided rides with an instructor do not require a license.

3. Can children ride in the buggies?

Children can ride in two-seater or four-seater buggies accompanied by an adult. Age restrictions may apply for certain packages.

4. What safety measures are included?

All rides come with helmets, seat belts, and safety briefings. Professional guides accompany you for added safety.

5. How long are the buggy rides?

Rides range from 30 minutes, 1 hour, to 2 hours, depending on the package selected. Longer rides are available for premium or group experiences.

6. Are refreshments provided?

Refreshments are included in certain packages, especially for extended rides and family/group adventures.

7. Can I book a buggy ride online?

Yes, you can book any buggy package directly through our website, via WhatsApp, or by contacting our customer service.

8. What should I wear for a buggy ride?

Wear comfortable, casual clothing and closed shoes. Sunglasses and sunscreen are recommended to protect against the sun and sand.
