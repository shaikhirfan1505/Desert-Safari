FAQs الأسئلة الشائعة

1. What is included in a Desert Safari package?

Our Desert Safari packages include thrilling dune bashing, camel rides, sandboarding, quad biking, traditional entertainment shows, and a delicious BBQ dinner or breakfast depending on the tour type.

2. Can I customize my Desert Safari experience?

Yes! You can customize your safari with options like VIP seating, private 4x4 rides, photography sessions, or combining morning and evening experiences for a full-day adventure.

3. Is transportation provided for the safari?

Most of our packages offer pickup and drop from your Dubai hotel or residence using 4x4 Land Cruisers, ensuring a comfortable and safe journey to the desert.

4. Are Desert Safaris safe for children and elderly?

Yes, our safaris are family-friendly. Some activities like dune bashing may have age or height restrictions. Always inform us of children or elderly passengers so we can ensure safety.

5. What should I wear for a Desert Safari?

We recommend light, breathable clothing, comfortable shoes, a hat, sunglasses, and sunscreen. Evenings can be cooler, so a light jacket may be useful.

6. When is the best time to visit the desert?

The ideal months for a Desert Safari in Dubai are October to March when temperatures are cooler. Morning safaris are perfect for sunrise views, while evening safaris offer stunning sunsets.

7. How do I book a Desert Safari in Dubai?

You can book directly on our website, contact us via WhatsApp or phone, or book through your hotel concierge. Early booking is recommended, especially during peak tourist seasons.

8. Are meals and refreshments included?

Yes. Evening and overnight safaris typically include a BBQ dinner with vegetarian and non-vegetarian options. Overnight safaris also include breakfast, and soft drinks are provided on all tours.
