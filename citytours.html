FAQs الأسئلة الشائعة

1. What types of City Tours do you offer?

We offer half-day, full-day, and custom City Tours including cultural tours, modern city highlights, desert-city combos, and VIP private tours.

2. How long does each City Tour last?

Half-day tours are around 3–4 hours, full-day tours last 6–8 hours, and customized tours vary based on your chosen itinerary.

3. Are City Tours suitable for families and children?

Yes, our City Tours are family-friendly. Vehicles are comfortable and child seats can be provided upon request.

4. What is included in the City Tours?

Tours usually include transportation, a professional guide, entrance fees to major attractions (if applicable), and refreshments for full-day packages.

5. Do I need to book City Tours in advance?

Yes, booking in advance is recommended to secure your preferred dates, especially during weekends and holiday seasons.

6. Can I customize my City Tour itinerary?

Absolutely! We offer fully customizable tours where you can select your preferred attractions, duration, and type of tour.

7. Are meals included in the City Tours?

Meals are not included in half-day tours but full-day and VIP tours may include lunch or refreshments, depending on the package.

8. How can I book a City Tour?

You can book directly via our website, WhatsApp, or by contacting our customer support. Instant confirmations are provided for all bookings.
